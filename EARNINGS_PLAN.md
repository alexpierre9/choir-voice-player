# Choir Voice Player - Earnings Plan

## Value Proposition
"Learn your part in minutes, not hours."
A dedicated tool for choir members to practice their specific parts (Soprano, Alto, Tenor, Bass) by uploading sheet music and isolating their voice.

## Business Model
**SaaS Subscription**: $9/month

### Tiers
1.  **Free Tier**
    -   1 Sheet Music Upload per month
    -   Basic Playback (All voices)
    -   Watermarked Audio

2.  **Pro Tier ($9/mo)**
    -   Unlimited Uploads
    -   Voice Isolation (Mute/Solo SATB)
    -   Download MIDI/MP3 of specific parts
    -   Priority Processing (Gemini Pro)
    -   Cloud Storage for Repertoire

## Market Strategy
1.  **Target Audience**:
    -   Choir Directors (Bulk licenses)
    -   Amateur Choir Members (Church choirs, Community choirs)
    -   Music Students

2.  **Acquisition**:
    -   SEO: "practice choir parts app", "sheet music to audio", "SATB player"
    -   Direct Outreach: Emailing church music directors.
    -   Social: TikTok/Reels showing "Before vs After" practice.

## Technical Costs vs Revenue
-   **Cost**:
    -   Gemini API: ~$0.01 per page (Vision).
    -   Hosting (Vercel/AWS/VPS): ~$20-50/mo fixed.
-   **Revenue**:
    -   100 users = $900/mo.
    -   Break-even at ~10 users.

## Roadmap to $1k MRR
1.  **Launch (Month 1)**: Deploy MVP. Post on r/choir, r/singing. Goal: 10 users.
2.  **Feedback (Month 2)**: Refine OMR accuracy. Add mobile-friendly player.
3.  **Growth (Month 3)**: Content marketing (blog posts on sight-reading). Goal: 100 users.
